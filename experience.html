USAA (Contract) - Enabling Compliant, Data-Driven Banking Campaigns at USAA

United States

Domain: Banking, Omnichannel Marketing, Compliance Analytics

Tools: SQL • Snowflake • Data Warehousing • Tableau • Reporting • Campaign Analytics • Cross-functional Collaboration • JIRA

Overview

As part of USAA’s Data Strategy team, I supported the execution, measurement, and infrastructure development of omni-channel marketing campaigns for banking products such as credit cards and deposit accounts. The focus was on delivering personalized and compliant marketing experiences through scalable, cloud-based data solutions.

Challenge & Context

While campaign goals and audience criteria were set by business teams, our responsibility was to operationalize those strategies using accurate, compliant, and efficient data pipelines. This included validating campaign audiences, enabling robust reporting, and ensuring that the data environment supported performance tracking across marketing channels.

My Approach

  • Data Infrastructure & Modeling: Developed and maintained a cloud-based marketing data warehouse. Co-designed optimized data models and enabled the flow of attributes from raw to reporting layers in collaboration with engineering teams using Snowflake.
  • Audience Validation & Compliance: Implemented SQL rules for the Audience Generation Process (AGP) and analyzed pre/post-campaign audience data to ensure compliance by excluding ineligible members during execution.
  • Campaign Reporting: Partnered with the BBIA reporting team to optimize SQL powering Tableau dashboards, significantly improving performance. Built reports to monitor credit card transactions and campaign engagement.
  • Documentation & Agile Collaboration: Drafted technical requirement documents, created user stories, and tracked deliverables in JIRA while collaborating across IT and business teams.
  • Data Quality: Analyzed gaps between source systems and target environments to improve data validation processes and reporting accuracy.

Impact

  • Improved campaign compliance by validating audience eligibility and optimizing execution rules.
  • Boosted reporting speed and reliability through efficient SQL and data model improvements.
  • Acted as a bridge between business stakeholders, analysts, and engineers to translate marketing goals into measurable outcomes.

Data & Analytics Engineer

Porter (Contract) – Data & Analytics Enablement for Healthcare E-Commerce

United States

Tools: SQL • Python • HCPCS/ICD Codes • AWS Athena • S3 • Freshpaint • Mixpanel • Azure DevOps • Supply Chain KPIs

Overview

Porter is a healthcare e-commerce platform focused on delivering durable medical equipment (DME) to patients through a drop-shipping model supported by Healthsphere. The goal was to enhance data analytics, streamline ordering workflows, and provide greater visibility into customer journeys and supplier performance.

Challenge & Context

Porter's raw medical claims and order data were siloed across systems like AWS Athena and Ultra Commerce, with limited tracking of digital user events or supply chain visibility. The team needed centralized analytics to enable operational metrics and future data warehousing initiatives.

My Approach

  • Claims Data Analysis: Preprocessed and performed gap analysis on medical claims using SQL and Python; queried data from S3 via Athena and validated HCPCS, ICD, and principal diagnosis codes.
  • Event Tracking & Analytics: Supported tagging/testing of customer journey events using Freshpaint and developed Mixpanel dashboards to monitor product views, purchases, and conversions.
  • Agile Collaboration: Authored user stories and coordinated tasks using Azure DevOps across engineering, operations, and analytics teams.
  • Supplier Coordination: Participated in supplier meetings to define integration needs and facilitate order fulfillment communication.
  • Supply Chain KPIs: Analyzed order data and contributed to defining metrics like perfect order rate, on-time delivery, and damage-free delivery.
  • Integration & Scalability: Worked with engineers integrating vendor and order data via Ultra Commerce, supporting future data warehouse initiatives.

Impact

  • Enabled efficient claims data access through Athena and improved code-level validation coverage for DME analysis.
  • Increased visibility into customer behavior using Mixpanel dashboards for leadership review.
  • Improved supply chain communication and KPI tracking for order processing and vendor coordination.
  • Established foundational analytics workflows supporting future growth in data warehousing and reporting automation.
